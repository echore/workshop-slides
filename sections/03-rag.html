Part 3 · RAG

What is RAG

Before answering, a RAG system retrieves relevant passages and adds them to the model's input. It can also show citations.

What the name means:

Retrieval

Find the relevant passages in your documents.

Augmented

Add those passages to the model's input.

Generation

Use the question and passages to write the answer.

It helps address the gaps from the warm-up: private files, newer information, and answers that need supporting sources.

Part 3 · RAG

How RAG works

A retriever finds relevant passages, adds them to the model's context, and the model writes an answer.

Question

You ask across your 10 papers.

→
Search

It searches by keywords, meaning, or both.

→
Passages

Pulls the few paragraphs that fit.

→
Add to context

Places those passages beside your question.

→
Grounded answer

Uses them to answer and can show sources.

Those passages enter the context window from Part 1. During normal RAG use, retrieval does not update the model's weights.
