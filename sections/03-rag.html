Part 3 · RAG

What is RAG

Before answering, the model looks it up in documents you give it, then answers from them with sources.

What the name means:

Retrieval

Find the relevant passages in your documents.

Augmented

Add them to what the model sees.

Generation

It writes the answer from those passages.

It closes the gaps the warm-up exposed: private files it never saw, updates after its cutoff, and claims with no source.

How RAG works

The model searches your documents, then answers from what it finds.

Those passages drop into the context window from Part 1. The model's training never changes.
